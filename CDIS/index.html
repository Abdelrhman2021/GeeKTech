About The Event

Egypt Cyber Security & Data Intelligence Systems (CDIS) is a 3-day cybersecurity and data intelligence systems conference and exhibition that will shed light on the status quo and future of cybersecurity in Egypt, MENA, and worldwide. International, regional, and local speakers will discuss and share their knowledge and expertise with attendees.

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

Egypt International Exhibition Center, Cairo

The Egypt International Exhibition Center in Cairo is a state-of-the-art facility that hosts a wide range of events, including conferences, exhibitions, and trade shows. Located in the vibrant city of Cairo, this spacious venue provides the perfect setting for our cybersecurity and data intelligence systems conference.


With modern amenities, ample space, and a central location, the Egypt International Exhibition Center offers a comfortable and convenient environment for attendees to explore the latest trends and technologies in cybersecurity and data intelligence.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the event's date and location?

    The event will take place on November 15-17, 2023, at the Egypt International Exhibition Center in Cairo.

  • How can I register for the event?

    You can register for the event by visiting our website and clicking on the "Register Now" button. Follow the instructions to complete your registration.

  • Is there a fee for attending the event?

    Yes, there is a registration fee to attend the event. Please refer to our registration page for pricing details and available discounts.

  • Can I bring a guest to the event?

    Guests are welcome! However, they will need to register separately and pay the registration fee to attend the event.

  • Are there any special accommodations for attendees with disabilities?

    Yes, we provide special accommodations for attendees with disabilities. Please contact our support team in advance to make necessary arrangements.

  • What should I bring to the event?

    We recommend bringing a valid ID, your registration confirmation, and any necessary materials for note-taking. Dress comfortably for a full day of networking and learning.
