    About Us

    At Project Electrify we are committed to creating a cleaner and more sustainable future for Egypt.

    Mission:

    Project Electrify is committed to retrofitting 2- and 3-wheel logistics vehicles into electric vehicles (EVs) to reduce spare parts and fuel costs, while providing cost savings, environmental friendliness, and improved vehicle performance. The company is dedicated to creating a cleaner and more sustainable future for Egypt.

    Vision:

    Project Electrify aims to be a leader in EV retrofit services in Egypt, providing innovative and accessible solutions that help individuals and businesses transition to more sustainable transportation options. The company strives to create a world where EVs are affordable and accessible to all, contributing to a healthier environment and a stronger economy. Project Electrify is committed to continuously improving its services and making a positive impact on its community and the world.

    Common Q&A

    Get answers to common questions about our electric vehicle conversion services and solutions.

    • 01 What is Electrify by GeeKTech and what is their goal?

      Electrify by GeeKTech is a project that specializes in retrofitting and converting ICE-powered vehicles to electric vehicles (EVs). Their goal is to provide affordable and sustainable transportation solutions for last-mile delivery and logistics companies and transportation, as well as for individuals looking to reduce their carbon footprint.

    • 02 What kind of components are used in Electrify's EV conversions?

      Electrify uses high-quality and reliable EV components to ensure the longevity and durability of their conversions. Their battery packs are designed to provide long-range and fast charging times, allowing for uninterrupted use of the vehicles.

    • 03 What other services does Electrify offer besides EV conversions?

      In addition to the conversion services, Electrify also offers a battery swap solution to provide a more convenient and efficient way for their customers to charge their EVs. They utilize fintech solutions for payments, allowing for seamless and secure transactions, whether it be cash, card, or buy-now-pay-later (BNPL).
