01

Sign up for an account

Start by signing up for an account with our money pool service. Provide your personal information and create a username and password.

02

Create or join a money pool

Once you have an account, you can either create a new money pool or join an existing one that suits your needs. You can also set up the pool's settings, such as the name, the target amount, and the deadline.

03

Invite friends and family to join

Invite your friends and family to join your money pool, or share a link to the pool on your social media accounts. The more people join the pool, the faster you can reach your target amount.

04

Set up payment method

Before the pool's deadline, make sure to set up your payment method to contribute to the pool. You can choose from various payment methods, such as credit cards or bank transfers.

05

Contribute to the pool

Once your payment method is set up, you can start contributing to the pool. You can make one-time or recurring contributions depending on your preference.

06

Track progress and withdraw funds

You can track the pool's progress and see how much is left to reach the target amount. Once the target amount is reached, the pool manager can withdraw the funds and distribute them to the pool's participants.
