GeeKTech and BraveNave Back Naem in Revit-Up Speed Race

GeeKTech, the leading tech company, has announced its sponsorship of Mr. Naem Al Arksousy in his upcoming speed race competition, "RevUp Speed Race." This sponsorship, in collaboration with BraveNave Media, will not only support Naem's passion for speed but also ensure that the media coverage of the event is top-notch.

Naem, who drives a turbo-charged 1.8-liter VW Beetle, is known for his incredible speed and driving skills. The sponsorship from GeeKTech will provide him with the latest technology and equipment to enhance his performance on the race track. This move by GeeKTech aligns with its vision of promoting innovation and supporting emerging talents in various fields.
