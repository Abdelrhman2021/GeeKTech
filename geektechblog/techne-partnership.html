GeeKTech Joins Forces with Techne Summit Alexandria 2022 to Foster Entrepreneurship and Innovation in Egypt

GeeKTech is proud to announce its partnership with Techne Summit Alexandria 2022, one of the largest events in Egypt related to entrepreneurship and startups. From November 5th to 8th, 2022, the summit brought together innovators, entrepreneurs, and investors from around the world to exchange ideas, network, and collaborate on new projects.

As a community partner, GeeKTech played a crucial role in spreading the word about the summit within its community and on social media platforms. The organization also shared information about the event through newsletters, providing its members with valuable insights into the latest developments in entrepreneurship and innovation.

Techne Summit Alexandria 2022 provided a unique platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas, connect with potential investors and partners, and gain exposure for their startups. With its focus on technology, entrepreneurship, and innovation, the summit offered a wealth of opportunities for attendees to learn, grow, and advance their careers.

For GeeKTech, the partnership with Techne Summit Alexandria 2022 was a natural fit. The organization is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and innovation in Egypt, and its mission is to support and promote the development of the tech community in the country. Through its partnership with Techne Summit, GeeKTech was able to further this mission and help drive growth and development in the tech industry.

In conclusion, GeeKTech is thrilled to have been a part of Techne Summit Alexandria 2022 and to have played a role in fostering entrepreneurship and innovation in Egypt. The organization looks forward to continuing its partnership with Techne Summit and to supporting the tech community in the country for many years to come.
