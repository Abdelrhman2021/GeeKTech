Data Harnessing & Mining Platform

The Project aims to construct the basis of a platform that can be used to provide all the data that an investor or entrepreneur may need to start a new project with a scope of focus on Egypt basically a platform that can be used as a main tool for investors, business owners and in our case study companies in the field of tourism with a focus on the South Sinai area to enable them to identify and make use of the opportunities available in the market, while also enabling companies to export their data in forms that can work with banks and help them in their work process to get all kinds of paper work needed for loans or letters of guarantee, we do so by allowing them to export their companies track record, history, list of assets, suppliers and client listings.
